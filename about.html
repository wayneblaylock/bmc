About Blue Man Cards

Life can be really hard. Especially when it feels like every step you take is met with criticism, and every effort you make seems inadequate. Unfortunately, these are common feelings in a world that loves to criticize.

Many are focusing especially on the flaws of men and masculinity. Granted, there are problems that need to be solved, and faults that need to be addressed. However, criticism without understanding will accomplish no good.

Hence: Blue Man Cards. We hope to remind the world that there are still good men, while pursuing our mission: “To inspire and enable men to become their best selves.” The evolution of this brand has been tremendous considering its beginning as a simple idea.

The Man Card

It was the beginning of my sophomore year of high school while I was working at a warehouse with a friend. We came up with an idea of making work more interesting by creating some Man Cards. I printed two out on cardstock and laminated them with packaging tape. We would bet our Man Card over competitions with each other.

The following summer, I took a coding class with a different friend (now the BMC co-founder). This is where the idea of a Man Card grew. We pieced together a janky website with the idea of selling Man Cards and creating a larger-scale trading game.

The website idea died off as we realized we were not capable of developing it, but through the rest of high school, I experimented with some different Man Card designs and sold them to friends and even in a few local stores.

The Brand

Years later, we started talking about it again, but with a different angle in mind. We didn't just want to sell cards; we wanted to build a brand with a purpose. Something that could really benefit men. We realized the Man Card could be more than just a novelty item; it could be a symbol of positive masculinity and a commitment to personal growth.

This time around, we were better equipped to build a website. More importantly, we were able to define our mission and work toward it. We do this by researching different topics that pertain to men, and writing about it.

You can find articles on our blog and content on our social media pages. And of course, we still sell Man Cards, both online and in retail stores.

If you'd like to support our brand and our mission, here's how you can get involved:

  • Check out our content: If you enjoy our articles and insights, share them with others.
  • Get a Man Card: For yourself, or as a gift.
  • Give us feedback or suggest topics: Visit our contact page to connect with us and be a part of the conversation. Share your thoughts, or suggest topics for us to explore.
